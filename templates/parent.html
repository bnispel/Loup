Where we ship

We can ship your products anywhere in the U.S. — and throughout North America, for that matter. We serve every major gateway to Mexico and interchange traffic with Canada. Factor in ocean carriers, and we can help you ship to virtually any destination worldwide.

How we ship

Our goal is to find the most efficient door-to-door solution to meet your goals. Oftentimes, we’ll combine the economic advantages of shipping by rail with the flexibility of over-the-road trucking, using affordable rail shipping for the long haul and trucks for final delivery. A process called transloading — moving products seamlessly between trucks and trains and back again — allows this to be possible. We can also move intermodal containers between modes.

Why Combine Shipping Modes?

Leveraging both trucks and trains allows you to lower transportation costs and access the benefits of rail. Typically, shipping by rail is a more affordable shipping option. However, many shippers — or receivers — don’t have tracks at their door. With transloading and intermodal options, it doesn’t matter: all shippers and receivers can benefit from rail.

A SOLUTION FOR JUST ABOUT EVERY PRODUCT

Moving products between trucks and trains requires specialized equipment. Our network of more than 600 transloaders, 85+ intermodal facilities and 60+ auto ramps has the equipment to safely transfer just about anything you need to ship:

  • Forklift

    For products like lumber, paper and palletized goods

  • CONVEYOR

    For bulk products like sand, soda ash, grains, flour and sugar

  • PUMP/PNEUMATIC TRANSFER

    For products like plastic pellets and liquids

  • CRANE

    For large, heavy products like steel beams, rebar, pipe and wind components

  • FRONT-END LOADER

    For products like rock, salt and other minerals and aggregates
