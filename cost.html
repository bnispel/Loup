Have Cost Concerns?

Let's stretch your transportation dollar.

We understand transportation is an expense you probably wish you didn’t have. We can help you stretch your transportation dollar — and make your experience seamless, too.

Our cost-saving solutions include:

  • Finding the most cost-effective transportation modes for your supply chain
  • Eliminating excess costs like demurrage
  • Reducing risks associated with handling and damage during transport

Whether you’re searching for the most efficient shipping mode or want to build a leaner supply chain, we’ll find the cost-saving opportunities you’re looking for.

What’s Your Challenge? Find Your Solution.

To learn how you can get solutions that address your cost challenges, find your challenge below.

I need to find more cost-effective ways to ship.

We can leverage multiple modes of transportation in a single supply chain. For instance, if you want to use a more efficient shipping mode like rail, we can pick up and/or deliver your products with trucks, then ship by rail for the long haul. The result? You save and your products still arrive safe and sound.

I have the right shipping modes in place, but I still need to save costs.

We can find ways to actually remove steps from your supply chain, while keeping the modes you use intact. For instance, if your product is shipped to a warehouse to be packaged, we can perform that service (and many other specialized services) onsite, saving the additional time and transportation cost.

My products have experienced damage in transit — I can’t keep incurring these costs.

There will always be risks when shipping, but we can help you reduce that risk by working with proven partners. Our suppliers are vetted to ensure your products are handled with care.

I want to ship by rail but I don’t want to pay to get tracks at my door.

Good news: You don’t have to have tracks at your door to use rail. Our transloaders and intermodal terminal operators safely move products between trucks and trains, so you can connect to rail.

We have a lot of excess costs for things like demurrage and PECS.

Additional costs are painful. Our logistics experts use technology and expertise to identify problems before they happen, so you can avoid these costs altogether.

Delivery delays are costing me money.

sing our ShipmentVision shipment tracking tool, you can see exactly where your products are — and if it looks like a problem is ahead, our logistics team can help avoid delays.

I wish I could better manage my inventory – putting too much in the pipeline gets costly.

You can! Our logistics services give you visibility to your product across rail carriers, providing customized reporting on how much product is in your pipeline and when it is scheduled to arrive. Using the ShipmentVision [link to SV page] Capacity Manager tool, you’ll have time to make the right decisions about inventory as well as greater control over your pipeline’s health.

I need to store my products, but buying land and building a warehouse isn’t realistic.

We have warehouses across the country that are ready to and waiting to store your products, saving you the capital investment. Even better? You can ship products to these warehouses by train, store them, then have trucks pick them up for on-demand delivery.

Can’t find your particular challenge? We know there are lots more out there. Get in touch and we can talk through a solution customized to your needs.
